<div id="content">
    <a name="content"></a> <div class="BannerBox"><font color="red">Scammers have been making phone calls claiming to represent the National Do Not Call Registry.  The calls claim to provide an opportunity to sign up for the Registry.  These calls are not coming from the Registry or the Federal Trade Commission, and you should not respond to these calls. To add your number to the Registry you can call 888-382-1222 from the phone you wish to register, or go click on “Register a Phone Number” in the left column of this page. 
 </font><br /><br /><b>Your registration will not expire. </b>Telephone numbers placed on the National Do Not Call Registry will remain on it permanently due to the Do-Not-Call Improvement Act of 2007, which became law in February 2008.  Read more about it at <a href="http://www.ftc.gov/opa/2008/04/dncfyi.shtm" target="_blank" accessskey="6">http://www.ftc.gov/opa/2008/04/dncfyi.shtm</a>.</div>
    <p class="title"><a href="/register/reg.aspx"><img src="images/NewDNCLogo.gif" alt="Click Here to Register Now" class="dnc" /></a>
WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW ABOUT<br /> 
THE NATIONAL DO NOT CALL REGISTRY</p>

<p>The National Do Not Call Registry gives you a choice about whether to receive telemarketing calls at home. <a href="http://www.ftc.gov/bcp/edu/pubs/consumer/alerts/alt107.shtm" target="_blank" accesskey="c" title="Exceptions to the National Do Not Call Registry">Most telemarketers</a>	should not call your number once it has been on the registry for 31 days. If they do, you can file a complaint at this Website. You can register your home or mobile phone for free.</p>
<div class="btnspace"><a id="lnkRegisterNow" title="Click Here to Register Now" class="registerButtons" href="/register/reg.aspx" target="_top">Register Now</a></div>
<p>Attention sellers and telemarketers: Go to <a href="https://telemarketing.donotcall.gov" title="https://telemarketing.donotcall.gov" accesskey="t">https://telemarketing.donotcall.gov</a> to subscribe to the National Do Not Call Registry.</p>
<p>If you are an exempt organization, and you wish to scrub your call lists, you may subscribe, but are not required to do so.</p>
