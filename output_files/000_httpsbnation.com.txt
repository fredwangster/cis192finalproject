  <h3><a href="http://www.sbnation.com/nba/2012/4/17/2954866/iman-shumpert-defines-swag">Iman Shumpert Defines 'Swag' And Its Recent&nbsp;Derivatives</a></h3>
  <p>This <a target="_blank" href="http://www.nypost.com/f/print/sports/knicks/serby_sunday_with_iman_shumpert_GxjzrpzNf68MU49B9HbtRN">whole New York Post interview</a> is just out of control in the best of ways.</p>
<blockquote>
<p><b>Q: Define "swag" for me.</b></p>
<p>A: Oh, I&rsquo;m off swag. I say "sway" now. I kept saying swag, swag.  Instead of saying bye on the phone I would say swag. I was saying it all  the time and being funny, and then like I wasn&rsquo;t even trying to be  funny anymore and I couldn&rsquo;t stop saying it. Then I changed s-w-a-g to  s-c-h-w-a-g just so I could sorta have my own thing. But then it was  just so many people saying it that I just got so sick of hearing it. ...  Kanye says it&rsquo;s cray instead of crazy in one of his songs, so I said,  "My swag is cray, so I&rsquo;m gonna call it sway." So now I sway. You don&rsquo;t  walk in the room swagging, I walk in swaying.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Iman Shumpert is the greatest. And he's single, ladies, but anyone who'd like to spend the rest of her life swayin' with Shump had better remember what he wants in a woman. "They have to be a taco-making maniac," he said.</p>
